Future Voters Academy is pleased to announce the launch of its official website at [futurevotersacademy.org](https://futurevotersacademy.org).

The site provides the public with clear, accessible information about FVA's mission, current and planned programs, a directory of nonpartisan civic resources, and details on how to donate, volunteer, or partner with the organization.

## What You'll Find on the Site

- **About** — Our mission, values, founder Thomas Cobb, and FAQ
- **Programs** — Community workshops, voter education materials, and civic literacy curriculum
- **Civic Resources** — A curated directory of official government sources and nonpartisan organizations
- **Contact** — How to reach FVA and request organizational documents
- **Donate** — Giving options and an honest notice about our current tax status

## What's Next

FVA is currently in the planning phase for its first public workshop series in Pierce and Ware Counties, Georgia. We are also actively pursuing formal 501(c)(3) nonprofit status.
